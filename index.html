Creating your product outline...

You're leading this — start by mapping your sections

Think about the journey your buyer takes through this product. What do they need to know first? What's the natural next step? Add each section below — you can reorder them as you go. Don't overthink it: 4–6 focused sections is usually perfect.

Not sure where to start?

Tell AI what your product covers and ask it to suggest some section ideas — you can pick and choose what fits.

Section ideas — click any to add it to your outline

Want AI to check your outline?

Once you've added your sections, AI can flag any blindspots, suggest a better order, or point out what might be missing.

How to edit your outline

  • Click any section title to rename it — just click and type
  • Drag the ≡ handle on the left to reorder sections
  • Click ✕ to remove a section you don't need
  • Use "Give feedback" below if you want AI to revise the whole outline
